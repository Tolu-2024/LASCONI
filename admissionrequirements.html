      The School Complex was established based on educational and professional Nursing Services required to meet the health needs of the ever growing and large population of Lagos State which has been put at over 20 million (Population Census 2012).

      Vision

      Provide opportunities for individuals who are interested in the Nursing profession to acquire skills for the purpose of providing quality Nursing care to individuals, families and communities at large.

      Mission

      To build a model Nursing Institution of Excellence in Nigeria producing graduates who are competent based on best global practices.

Admission Requirements

Mission

To build a model Nursing Institution of Excellence in Nigeria producing graduates who are competent based on best global practices.

Philosophy

Our Philosophy & Objectives Lagos State School of Nursing Complex is dedicated to the training of professional Nurses who will provide the highest quality clients care in a compassionate and personalized manner within the local social, cultural and economic environment.

LIST OF REQUIREMENTS FOR SUCCESSFUL CANDIDATES COMING INTO THE COLLEGE HOSTEL

  1. Laptop
  2. Two (2) pairs of Black (flat-soled) covered shoes
  3. Three (3) white short sleeve uniforms with side pockets/ trousers and tops for men
  4. One (1) school bag
  5. One (1) Rechargeable lamp/ Torchlight
  6. One (1) Plastic Bucket
  7. One (1) Mosquito treated net
  8. Antiseptic/ Disinfectant (Dettol/ Izal)
  9. One (1) pair of stud earrings (optional)
  10. Two (2) Plates/ Spoon/ Cup
  11. Bathing Soap/ Laundry soap/ Detergent
  12. Sponge/ Soap case (No native sponge)
  13. A minimum of three (3) pairs of black and white underwear/ tight/ boxers for boys
  14. Beverages
  15. Body cream/ Lotion/ hair cream
  16. Sewing kit (nail cutter, thread, and needles)
  17. One (1) Nurse’s watch
